OUR MARKETING STRATEGY




DIGITAL MARKETING STRATEGY

In order to achieve success in the digital marketplace, your web presence must attract attention, communicate effectively, and convert fluid. As leading digital marketing strategy consultants, Zugcic's goal is to increase traffic through and integrated digital marketing strategy and to increase engagement through world class design & user experience.


DIGITAL MARKETING DISCOVERY PROCESS

TECHNICAL SITE ANALYSIS


Zugcic marketing will evaluate the site’s frontend for search. Frontend points of analysis include navigation and textual content. All technical gaps will be outlined in a detailed plan over the course of our partnership.

KEY PHRASE STRATEGY


The Key phrase Strategy is important to have successful digital marketing strategy. Zugcic digital marketing will research the words & phrases that consumers are using when searching for your products and services. These key phrases are then strategically categorized to create a clear focus. With this structure, we can create a clear plan that targets specific key phrases that will increase authority, rankings, and traffic.

DEVELOPMENT


With a completed Key phrase Strategy, we can now begin the development. Each set of key phrases are properly categorized so that we can create content that aligns with consumer expectations. This content strategy is added as feature in this process.

CONVERSION ANALYSIS


To truly measure campaign success, the site must be able to track and measure those user actions that would be considered goals, or conversions, that are key to the company. Depending on the brand’s overall goals and industry, Zugcic will recommend a series of conversion tracking implementation to not only measure success, but also gain deeper understanding into the path of conversion for the brand’s audience.
