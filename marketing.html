BRANDING AND IDENTITY HAVE CHANGED

Since beginning branding and identity were connected with company name and logo. Today, our designers have been charged with a more complex definition. Social networks are one of the most popular space to promote identity of company. From social media comments there are so many opportunities to tarnish a company’s brand and identity.

When customer hears company's name, identity and brand must be first that comes to his mind. It’s a mental image of what a company represents. As consumer behaviors and expectations change, one thing that won’t change is the impact that he has on your success.

THE IMPORTANCE OF BRANDING

Strategic branding differentiates you from competitors, and then positions you as the leader in the business space. Successful branding starts with knowing your customers needs. The deeper your understanding their needs the more is effecting.

Knowing their needs you’ll be more successful to convince them to choose you. Investigating, outlining, and modelling brand is invaluable in today’s crowded digital marketing world.

LOGO DESIGN AND IDENTITY

Instant brand recognition is founded in the company’s logo. Some organizations are willing to spend whatever it takes to have a legendary logo because they know the weight it carries.

A logo serves as the face of the company and represents its identity. In addition, it is the synonym for a company’s brand colors, designs, and fonts. Zugcic marketing can help you deliver an exclusive identity by designing an exclusive logo and related marketing materials.

THE IMPACT OF A CREATIVE LOGO

Appearing on business cards, websites, and social media networks, a powerful logo can make or break a conversion. An amateur looking logo can quickly turn off your customers. It can represent your organization in a poor light. If you are investing in high-quality creative service your consumers will notice.
